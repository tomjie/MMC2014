Why people migration?

Why do people want to emigrate? what is the primary factor considered by immigrants? Our map aims at displaying the trend and features of immigration in 2010, thus leading to some useful knowledge in this field. Moving points are used to represents the Immigration direction, number of points indicate number of immigrants. The basemap gives reasons immigration happened, which can be chosen by clients themselves.
